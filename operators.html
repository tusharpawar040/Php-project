David Guetta

Pierre David Guetta (/ˈɡɛtə/ GET-ə, French: [pjɛʁ david ɡeta]; born 7 November 1967) is a French DJ,
record producer, musician and songwriter. He has over 50 million record sales globally,
with more than 10 billion streams.[2] In 2011, 2020 and 2021, Guetta was voted the number
one DJ in the DJ Mag Top 100 DJs poll.[3] In 2013,
Billboard crowned "When Love Takes Over" as the number one dance-pop collaboration of all time.[4]
